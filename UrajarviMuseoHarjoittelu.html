Description

The game has a short story that takes some liberties from reality to tell the story of the brother and the sister living in the mansion. In the story Lilly and Hugo prepare for dinner with the ladys from the womens assosiation and decide to make hold a play in their home. Things start disappearing and rooms become a mess. At the end it is revealed that a ghost is responsible for the mayhem. the ghost is called sofia and she was the maid for the mansion before dying. The real museum is said to have a ghost that appears to visitors and staff. During the time that the siblings were alive at their mansion it was a cultural center where allsorts events happend.

Features

The game has six levels. Each level has three items that can be found. When you complete the level you get an item and it gets harder because next time you have to find ten more items. There are three difficulty levels. The game has a two minute timelimit. In total there are eighteen items that can be found. Those items are on show in the museum.
