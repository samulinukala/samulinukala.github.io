Project list
Flappyboy

This game is a copy of Flappybird. In this game you can switch your bird between three choices. I was responsible making pipe spawner, start menu, end menu,bird switch,highscore system and background. The game is made with Unity.

The ball Game

In this game your objective is to get through the maze as fast as possible to the goal. The game tells you your time and your fastest time. You control a ball with wasd. The game is made with unity.

Flies

In this game you fly a UFO trying to collide with characters walking on the ground randomly. You must dodge volley of bullets fired at you. The level takes place in a bedroom frozen in time in the middle of a ghost attack. The game is made in Unreal Engine.

Get to the beach

Your objective is to run away from the darkness trying to consume you. Level design switches half way through the level from a city to a forest. You must move precisely because there are mines everywere. The game was made during a gamejam. The gamejams theme was escape.

Urajärven kartanon mysteeri

Game made for a museum located in Urajärvi. In this game your task is to find objects in a picture based on the rooms in the museum. At the end of the levels the player is rewarded with an artifact.

Border Gods

The game is developed for Rajamuseo located in Imatra. Rajamuseo is a museum about border control in Finland. Game teaches children about border control and the importance of taking care of their documents. Game has some fantasy elements, gods from allkinds of regions around the world appear trying to get in to Finland and the player has to check their documents. The game rewards speed and accuracy. The player has two minutes of time to check the passports. The game will be part of the exhibition at the museum.

Zron Light Bike Races

This game was made during a gamejam. The gamejams theme was Arcade. I saw the movie Tron and I think there was a game similar to to the one that was made during the gamejam. In this game there are two modes singleplayer, frenzy and multiplayer. In single player you try to collect coins without dying. In frenzymode you try to survive as long as possible. In multiplayer you fight against a human player trying to make each other hit the trails.

Gaesarin Passit

The game is developed for Rajamuseo located in Imatra. Rajamuseo is a museum about border control in Finland. In this game you make your own passport. You can customize your picture, emblem, background and input your age and name.
