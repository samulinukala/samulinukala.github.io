They were made for an idea for a drawing doll. That could help me better understand how to draw people better. I think it is just something that I don't like to do that much, but still a useful skill to have. I have no real passion for modelling. I might get a passion for it if i learned it more. Studying modelling helps to understand how to use the models in games. These were suppoed to be props for the app. The project didn't really start any further than that.
